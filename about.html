About SR&Sons

Since Generations

Three generations of
goldsmiths, one workshop.

SR&Sons Jewellers began as a small family workbench and grew into a name trusted for hand-finished gold and gemstone craftsmanship. Every ring, chain, and bangle that leaves our workshop is inspected, weighed, and hallmarked before it reaches you.

We believe jewelry should mean something — which is why alongside our ready collection, a large part of what we do is building custom pieces from a customer's own idea, sketch, or family heirloom.

Origin
Founded as a small goldsmith workbench, working purely on referral and word of mouth.
Growth
Expanded into a full workshop offering casting, stone-setting, and polishing under one roof.
Today
A trusted name for both ready collections and fully bespoke, made-to-order jewelry.
