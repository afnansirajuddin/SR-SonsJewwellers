What We Offer

Our services

From made-to-order bespoke pieces to caring for jewelry you already own, our goldsmiths handle it all under one roof.

Bespoke Design

Bring a sketch, photo, or idea and we'll draft, quote, and hand-craft a one-of-a-kind piece with you at every step.

Heirloom Redesign

Turn old or inherited gold into a fresh design — same metal, reworked into a piece you'll actually wear.

Repair & Resizing

Ring resizing, clasp repair, re-polishing, and stone re-setting, handled in-house by our own goldsmiths.

Certified Hallmarking

Every finished piece — ready-made or custom — is weighed, tested, and hallmarked before it leaves our workshop.

Valuation & Consultation

Free, no-obligation valuation and design consultation for anyone exploring a purchase, gift, or redesign.

Bridal & Occasion Sets

Full matching sets — necklace, earrings, and tikka — designed and finished together for weddings and events.
